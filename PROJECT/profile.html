About Us

Root Flower is a dedicated florist based in Kuching, Malaysia. In addition to create beautiful floral products for customer orders, the company also provide interesting floral workshops. These workshops cover a range of skill such as hand-tied bouquets, flower arrangements, and more. The aim of the business is connecting the customers and administration support through a simple yet attractive website
